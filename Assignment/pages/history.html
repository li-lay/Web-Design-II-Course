Our History

A legacy of luxury and hospitality since 2000

The Founding Story

AKU Hotel began as a dream of our founder, Mr. Kim Saya, who envisioned creating a sanctuary of luxury in the center of Phnom Penh. After traveling the world and experiencing the finest hotels, he returned to Cambodia with a mission to create something truly special.

In 2000, with just 12 rooms in a restored colonial building, AKU Hotel opened its doors. Despite the modest beginnings, Mr. Kim's commitment to exceptional service and attention to detail quickly earned the hotel a reputation among discerning travelers.

Milestones
